An unconventional path to the Senate

Notre Dame grad appointed to second New Jersey seat after the death of Lautenberg

By: Nicole Michels
September 13, 2013


With the passing of senator Frank Lautenberg, a seat in the senate opened up. The person that was asked to fill this seat was, Notre Dame grad, Jeff Chiesa. Chiesa graduated from Notre Dame in 1987. He became the first Republican to hold a New Jersey senate sear since 1982.
